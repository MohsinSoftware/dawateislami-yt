What is the Quran?

The Quran is the holy book of Islam, believed to be the word of Allah as revealed to the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) over a period of 23 years. It is written in Arabic and consists of 114 chapters (Surahs).

Quranic Verse

"Indeed, this Quran guides to that which is most suitable..." - Surah Al-Isra (17:9)
