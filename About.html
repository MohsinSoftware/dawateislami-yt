What is Islam?

Islam is the belief in one God (Allah) and follows the teachings of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). It emphasizes peace, submission to Allah, and following the five pillars of Islam.

The Five Pillars of Islam

  • Shahada: Declaration of faith
  • Salat: Prayer
  • Zakat: Charity
  • Sawm: Fasting
  • Hajj: Pilgrimage
