Hadith of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

Hadith are sayings, actions, and approvals of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). They provide guidance for Muslims in various aspects of life.

Example of a Hadith:

"The best of you are those who are the most beneficial to others." - Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)
